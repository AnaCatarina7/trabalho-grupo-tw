Olivia Rodrigo

TV Girl is an American indie pop band from San Diego, California, consisting of Brad Petering, Jason Wyman, and Wyatt Harmon. The group is currently based in Los Angeles. TV Girl's 2012 mixtape, "The Wild, The Innocent, The TV Shuffle," was released and distributed for free along with a downloadable coloring book. This mixtape was intended to be their debut album, but the band expressed that it didn't feel official enough to be their first album, and they believed the term "mixtape" was more appropriate.
