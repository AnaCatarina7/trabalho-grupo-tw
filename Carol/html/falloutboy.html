Fall Out Boys

Fall Out Boy is a pop punk band formed in 2001 in the city of Wilmette, Illinois. The band consists of Patrick Stump, Pete Wentz, Andy Hurley, and Joe Trohman. The band has won several awards for their album "From Under the Cork Tree." Released in 2005, the album achieved double platinum certification after selling 2.7 million copies in the United States. In February 2007, the band released "Infinity on High" to greater success on the music charts, reaching #1 on the Billboard 200 and selling 260,000 copies in the first week. The first single, "This Ain't a Scene, It's an Arms Race," reached #1 on the Pop 100 and #2 on the Billboard Hot 100.
