Troye Sivan

Troye Sivan Mellet is a singer, songwriter, actor, and YouTuber born in South Africa and naturalized Australian. As an actor, he portrayed the young Wolverine in the film X-Men Origins: Wolverine, starred in the South African Spud trilogy, and appeared in the film Boy Erased. Since 2014, Troye has been exclusively dedicated to his music career, releasing EPs such as TRXYE, Wild, and In a Dream, as well as studio albums Blue Neighbourhood and Bloom. On August 7, 2010, he came out as gay to his family, and on August 7, 2013, Troye publicly came out in a video on his YouTube channel. Since then, he has been active as an LGBT activist.
