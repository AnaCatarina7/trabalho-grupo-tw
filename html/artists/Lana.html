Lana Del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, better known as Lana Del Rey, is an American singer, songwriter, model, and poet. Her music has been noted by critics for its stylized cinematic quality, themes of tragic romance, glamour, and melancholy, and references to pop culture, particularly American culture in the 1950s and 1960s. Nominated for various Grammy and Golden Globe awards, in 2021, she was named "Artist of the Decade" at the Variety Hitmakers Awards, hailed as "one of the most influential singers and songwriters of the 21st century."
