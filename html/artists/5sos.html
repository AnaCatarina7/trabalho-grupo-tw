5 Seconds of Summer

5 Seconds of Summer is an Australian pop-punk band formed in Sydney in 2011. The group consists of Luke Hemmings, Calum Hood, Michael Clifford, and Ashton Irwin. The band gained fame through YouTube, where they posted videos of themselves covering songs by various artists around 2011. The band's official debut single was released in February 2014 and is titled "She Looks So Perfect." The song topped the charts in Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, and the United Kingdom. Their self-titled debut studio album was released in June 2014 and reached #1 on the Billboard 200 in the United States and in more than 10 other countries.
