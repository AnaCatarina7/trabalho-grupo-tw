Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is an American singer, songwriter, actress, director, and screenwriter. Her narrative compositions, often inspired by her personal experiences, have garnered extensive media coverage and critical acclaim. Swift moved to Nashville at the age of 14 to pursue a career as a country music singer, signing a songwriting deal with Sony/ATV Music Publishing in 2004 and a recording contract with Big Machine Records in 2005, and with Republic Records in 2019.
