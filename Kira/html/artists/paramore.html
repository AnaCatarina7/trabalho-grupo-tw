Paramore

Paramore is an American rock band formed in Franklin, Tennessee in 2004. The band has released six studio albums: All We Know Is Falling, Riot!, Brand New Eyes, Paramore, After Laughter, and This Is Why. The lineup remained virtually unchanged from 2004 to 2010, when two founding members departed. The current members are Hayley Williams, Taylor York, and Zac Farro. The band's debut album, All We Know Is Falling, was released on July 26, 2005, reaching the second position on the Billboard Comprehensive Albums chart and earning gold certification in the UK for over 100,000 copies sold. The second album, Riot!, released on June 12, 2007, marked the group's first major success, selling over two million copies. Riot! also received platinum certification from the Billboard 200 for surpassing one million sales.
