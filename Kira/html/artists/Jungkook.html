JungKook

Jeon JungKook, often credited in his musical career simply as JungKook, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, dancer, and music producer. Jungkook gained worldwide prominence as a member of the male group BTS, formed in 2013. The group has sold over sixteen million albums in South Korea alone in physical sales, according to the Gaon Music Chart, and has become the act with the highest sales debut in the 2010s decade.
